My english video

Video script

Hi, my name is Elian Guilleminot and I will explain to you why I chose a degree in computer networking and telecommunications. This passion started when I was young and I saw my dad working on his computer and when I saw him open a Command-Line Interface (CLI), for me it was like magic because he could chat with the computer, and it answered him.

Another of my motivations is to understand the world and especially how new technologies work. For example, since I have been young, I regularly play online multiplayer video games and networks are very important to be able to play properly. That's why I like to understand how it works and maybe I can improve it in the future with my work.

My last and most important motivation is to make the world a safer place. As we can see, cyber security is a profession of the future. Attacks on hospital computer systems are a prime example. World War three could be virtual, that's why I want to help counter attacks from cyber criminals.
